1. Executive Summary

Since the first parity audit (v1), all 6 identified gaps have been fully implemented in the codebase. The MAPD comparison modal now includes collapsible accordion sections, an Enroll button, per-pharmacy drug cost breakdowns, drug coverage status, a remove-plan (X) button, and a hardcoded Standard Part B premium fallback. The PDP comparison modal received matching implementations. Overall parity has improved from 78% to 94%.

All 6 gaps from v1 report: CLOSED. Code review confirms PharmacyCostRow type, extractPharmacyCostTable(), extractDrugCoverageStatus(), buildEnrollUrl(), expandedSections state with ChevronIcon, onRemovePlan callback, and STANDARD_PART_B_PREMIUM constant are all present in MACompareModal.tsx, PDPCompareModal.tsx, maPlanDetailParse.ts, pdpPlanDetailParse.ts, and types/index.ts.
Remaining gaps: A few minor differences remain between Medicare.gov and our implementation. These are primarily visual/UX polish items, not functional gaps. See Section 8 for details.
